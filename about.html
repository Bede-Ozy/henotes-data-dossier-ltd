Our Story

Welcome to Henotes Data Dossier. We are a brand focused on building and fostering a strong data culture in the workplace. As a data capability and analytics enablement company, we help organisations make better decisions using data.

Nigeria and Africa are yet to fully tap into the vast advantages that data offers. Most first-world countries invested heavily in data-driven thinking, and this mindset accelerated technological advancement.

Our mission is to build globally capable data teams through practical skills, efficient processes, and cost-effective solutions. We believe that if data becomes a culture at work, it can become a culture in society.
