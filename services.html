Our Data Services

We empower organizations with practical, scalable, and cost-effective solutions to build a data-capable workforce and workflow.

Henotes Data Dossier provides a range of data capability and analytics services designed to help organisations move from fragmented reporting to confident, data-informed decision making.

Our services are modular and flexible. Organisations can engage with a single service or adopt a structured progression based on their current maturity, goals, and constraints.

Data Capability Assessment

Understand where your organisation stands today and what must change to improve data-driven decision making.

Data Literacy & Training

Equip teams at all levels with practical data skills using tools they already work with, from Spreadsheets to BI platforms.

Analytics & BI Enablement

Design dashboards, reports, and analytics workflows that support real business decisions, not just visuals.

Data Governance & Strategy

Implement governance frameworks and strategies to ensure data integrity, compliance, and long-term usability.

Reporting & Insights

Deliver actionable insights through custom dashboards, reports, and analytics workflows designed for your business goals.

How Organisations Typically Engage

Most of our engagements start with a simple but crucial step: understanding how data is currently being used across the organisation. We don’t just look at tools or reports; we explore how people interact with data, the processes in place, and where gaps or opportunities exist.

From there, we partner with teams to strengthen foundational skills, improve analytics delivery, and provide leadership with decision-focused insights that truly drive action.

How Our Services Fit Together

HDD’s services are designed to operate as an integrated system rather than as a collection of separate offerings. Each piece complements the others, creating a full-circle approach to building data capability.

It begins with a data capability assessment, which identifies strengths, gaps, and priorities. That insight informs our training programs, equipping teams with the skills they need to turn data into meaningful action.

Who Our Services Are For

Our services are built to support organisations at any stage of their data journey:

  • Leadership teams seeking clarity, confidence, and alignment in decision-making.
  • Operational teams looking to improve reporting, analysis, and performance tracking.
  • Data and analytics teams aiming to deliver insights that are not only accurate but also impactful.
